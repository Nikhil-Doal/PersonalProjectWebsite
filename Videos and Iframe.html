Videos

Videos can be a great way to make your website more interactive

To add a video to your webpage, you need to use the video tag

Heres how it works

<video width="320" height="240" controls autoplay muted>

<source src="movie.mp4" type="">

Fallback text

</video>

Fallback text is necessary in case the video does not play. It is like alt text.

HTML Video Formats

There are three supported video formats: MP4, WebM, and Ogg. The browser support for the different formats is:
